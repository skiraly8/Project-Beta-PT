About Me

I am a third-year Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT) student at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas with a strong interest in both orthopedic and sports rehabilitation, particularly with a focus on climbing injuries. I have a passion to not only maximize the potential of my patients, but to also be a top-tier clinician in the field.

Beyond my clinical rotations, I am a collaborator with The Climbing Doctor, where I help produce rehab and prehab content for the @theclimbingdoctor Instagram community. My work includes a featured article on hypermobility for the Climbing Doctor website and a guest appearance on his podcast, discussing specialized strategies for climbers.

Based in Seattle, I focus on empowering athletes through specific loading protocols and movement education to ensure they don't just recover, but improve.

Outside of physical therapy, I am an avid trail runner, climber, cyclist, snowboarder, traveler, backpacker, and enjoy all things outdoors.
